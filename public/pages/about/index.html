ABOUT CALIBER.

Executive black car service built on Long Island — not a national franchise, not a rideshare algorithm. Real dispatchers, real chauffeurs, real accountability.

Where are you based?

We are family owned and operated on Long Island, New York, serving Nassau, Suffolk, NYC, New Jersey, Connecticut, and Westchester.

How do I book?

Book online through our Moovs reservation link, call (516) 595-2391, or email info@calibercarservice.com. Same-day availability is subject to fleet schedule — call for urgent trips.

Do you only do airports?

No. Airports are a core specialty, but we also handle corporate travel, hourly as-directed service, Manhattan runs, weddings and events, and cruise terminals.

Can I request the same chauffeur?

We note preferences on your account when possible. Chauffeur assignment depends on schedule and vehicle class for each trip.
