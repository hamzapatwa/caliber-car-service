Which airport is best from Great Neck?

LGA is usually closest for domestic travel. JFK for most international. HPN when carrier and schedule fit — we help you choose when you book with flight details.

Do you serve all North Shore villages?

Yes — Garden City, Great Neck, Manhasset, Munsey Park, Flower Hill, Port Washington, and adjacent Nassau communities.

How long to Midtown Manhattan from Manhasset?

Typically 35–55 minutes off-peak; rush hour can extend that. We set pickup times accordingly.

Can I set up a corporate account for our Melville office?

Yes — many North Shore companies use corporate billing for executives commuting to Manhattan or airports. Call dispatch to open an account.
