WHITE PLAINS AIRPORT.

Westchester County Airport — the smart choice for northern Nassau, Suffolk, and Connecticut travelers. Fixed-rate service, live flight tracking, and a chauffeur who knows HPN inside and out.

How long does it take from Long Island to White Plains Airport?

Typically 45–90 minutes depending on your pickup location and traffic. Northern Nassau pickups are often under an hour; eastern Suffolk may take longer. We confirm a precise pickup window at booking.

Why choose HPN over JFK or LaGuardia?

Westchester County Airport is ideal for travelers based in northern Nassau, western Suffolk, or Connecticut who want a smaller terminal, shorter security lines, and competitive fares on select domestic routes — especially JetBlue and American.

Do you monitor my flight for delays?

Yes. We track every flight in real time. If your flight is delayed, arrives early, or changes gates, your driver adjusts automatically — at no extra charge.

Is there a wait fee if my flight is delayed?

No. Your 45-minute complimentary wait starts from actual landing time. If your flight is substantially delayed, we stay in contact and won't charge a penalty.

Can I book an HPN ride same-day?

Yes, subject to availability. For urgent or last-minute bookings, call our dispatch line directly at (516) 595-2391 rather than booking online.

Are your HPN rates truly flat — no tolls on top?

Yes. The rate you receive at booking is the rate you pay. Tolls and bridge fees are included unless otherwise noted at confirmation.
